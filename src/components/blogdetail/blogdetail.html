What is Moringa?

Moringa oleifera, often referred to as the "miracle tree," is a plant that has been praised for its health benefits for thousands of years. It is very rich in healthy antioxidants and bioactive plant compounds. Native to the Indian subcontinent, it thrives in the varied climates of Africa, making it a sustainable superfood source.
